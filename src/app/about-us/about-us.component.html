About NFT Finch

Who we are and how we started

NFTFinch team has got vast experience in technology, almost 50 years collectively, and a significant experience in driving business. We have always been keen on discussing new ideas that may contribute to how technology facilitates different parts of society. The birth of NFTFinch was an outcome of one of those productive discussions. We strongly believe that NFTFinch will change the way NFTs are seen in a major group of artists who are not technical but want to participate in NFT marketplaces. If you are an artist and want to publish your NFTs without knowing nuances of it then NFTFinch is the best place for you.

We tried to make NFTFinch as simple and intuitive as possible so that you don’t have to be tech-savvy for diving into this growing metaverse. You can start getting financial rewards for your artwork in a very few and easy steps.

Our Vision

Our vision is to facilitate talented artists sell their artworks in NFT marketplaces without even understanding what NFTs are. Well, they don’t have to, but they are welcome to understand how it works technically.

How It Works

Its very easy to get started with our platform –

Sign up or create your account on NFTFinch

Upload your artwork

Automatic minting and publishing of NFT on to leading marketplaces.

You get notification once your artwork is sold.

Money starts reflecting in your account.
